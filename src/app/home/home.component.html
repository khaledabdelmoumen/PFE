High-Tech Ceramic Filter Cartridge
Our water tap purifier boasts an impressive ceramic filtration system with a precision of 0.3 microns, ensuring the removal of a wide range of impurities. From sediment and rust to red worms, parasites, and harmful algae, our filtration technology leaves no room for contaminants. But it doesn't stop there – we enhance the taste by incorporating activated carbon granules, effectively eliminating chlorine and further improving the water's quality. With antibacterial granules that eliminate harmful bacteria, your water is not only crystal clear but also refreshingly sweet. Experience the epitome of water purity with Dracoss.

Easy Maintenance
Maintaining your water tap purifier is a breeze with Dracoss. Our high-quality filter cartridges typically last between 3-6 months, depending on the water conditions in your specific area.
