Greywater is wastewater from household activities that do not involve human waste. It typically comes from showers, baths, washing machines, and hand basins. Greywater is less contaminated than blackwater, which comes from toilets, kitchen sinks, and floor drains. Greywater can be reused for non-potable purposes, such as watering plants, flushing toilets, and washing clothes.

Characteristic Greywater Blackwater
Source Showers, baths, washing machines, hand basins, kitchen sinks Toilets, floor drains
Contaminants Low levels of bacteria and organic matter High levels of bacteria, viruses, and other pathogens
Reuse Can be reused for non-potable purposes Cannot be reused

Health & Safety

Dracoss Tap Water Filters are designed with safety as our highest priority. The filters are composed of materials that are safe for contact with drinking water. Furthermore, they effectively remove impurities and harmful contaminants from your tap water. However, they should be used and maintained as per the instructions provided to ensure their efficacy. If you have a specific health concern or condition, it's always a good idea to consult with your healthcare provider.

Dracoss filters are designed to remove a wide range of common contaminants found in tap water, including chlorine, heavy metals such as lead, and microbial contaminants like bacteria. Our filters use advanced filtration technologies to ensure that your water is safe and healthy for consumption. However, it's important to note that while our filters are highly effective, no filter can remove 100% of all possible contaminants, especially those at very low levels. For contaminants like certain industrial chemicals or pharmaceuticals, which might be present in some water sources, specific filtration methods may be required. If you're concerned about a specific contaminant in your local water supply, we recommend you get your water tested. Once you have the results, our customer service team would be more than happy to help you understand whether our filters are the right solution for you.
